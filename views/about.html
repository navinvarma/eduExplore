eduExplore

eduExplore is a free, not-for-profit web application ("app") that is provided as a service to students all over the world who wish to study in the United States of America.

This app provides prospective students the ability to:

  • Search various programs to study.
  • View information gathered from public online documents of Universities and Colleges. This includes College contact information, hyperlinks, degree eligibility, requirments and other relevant information.
  • Compare data points that pertain to each program offered. The data points include minimum eligibility, class profiles, tuition, aid and cost of living.
The information contained in this app is crowd sourced and cannot be relied upon as "official" information to make informed decisions. Please use the hyperlinks provided within the app to view up-to-date data to check if any descrepencies exist.

Privacy Policy

The information found in "eduExplore" is publicly available.
